  Trust

Trust is at the centre of all communities, and Ride Sharing is no exception. On Ride Sharing, every time two members meet in real life they publicly rate each other, allowing the members to build up a trusted community reputation. This means that before you travel with another member you can read their ratings and benefit from the experience of other members.

  Safety & Insurance

There are three important ways in which we create the environment for a secure ridesharing community. First, member identities are authentic. Second, the community self-moderates, using profiles and ratings to evaluate members and vet those that do not respect the spirit of positive community collaboration. Third,it has a dedicated Member Relations team, available every day to support users, so you to always get the best experience out of Ride Sharing.

  Choice

You get to choose your travelling companions. Co-travellers contact car owners of their choice, and in turn, car owners can quickly accept or decline requests. To help members decide, in addition to ratings, Ride Sharing provides valuable information about each members recent activity and experience level. This means you will always be confident and comfortable planning a rideshare.

  Confidentiality

BlaBlaCar needs your phone number to verify your identity and to send you notifications when you plan a rideshare. But this does not mean anyone will be calling you on it! Whether you prefer to receive emails or just do not have time to take calls, enabling the Hidden Number Option means your number will NOT be shown to members on your profile. And thanks to our internal messaging service, you can talk to any member without ever disclosing your email address either.
