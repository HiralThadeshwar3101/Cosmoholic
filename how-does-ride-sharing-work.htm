How it works

  • Do You Need To Travel?› Find a ride with a trusted car owner
  • Do You Have Empty Seats?› Offer a ride to co-travellers

Find a ride with a trusted car owner and save money on your travel, even at the last minute.

  1. 1. Find a ride

    Just enter your departure and arrival points and your travel date, then choose a car owner offering a ride going your way. Take a look at the Ride Comment for information about the car owner, their reasons for travelling and other journey details.

    Before getting in touch, you can check the trusted profile of the car owners, with a photo, bio and member ratings. Trust & Safety.

  2. 2. Get in touch

    Click on Contact Car owner and send a private message to the car owner to book your seat with them. You'll then get their phone number.

    If you send a private message, BlaBlaCar will immediately notify the car owner of your message via SMS and send your phone number, if you've opted to make it visible. This is a free service.

  3. 3. Travel together

    Be at your planned meeting point on time. Bring exact change to pay the car owner the agreed contribution during the ride.

    After travelling together leave a rating for your car owner, they will return the favour and it will boost your Experience Level. Car owners prefer travelling with co-travellers who have a high Experience level.

Rent your empty seats to trusted co-travellers, and share the costs of your city-to-city car journeys with people just like you!

  1. 1. Offer Your Ride Online

    Add your departure and arrival points, the date and time of your departure, and the price per co-traveller. Fill out the Ride Comment to give more information about you and the details of your journey.

    BlaBlaCar automatically suggests a fair, mileage-based price per co-traveller for you.

  2. 2. Your Co-travellers Contact You

    Co-travellers will contact you to confirm journey details by private message or phone. If they send a private message we will notify you immediately by SMS.

    You can view your co-travellers' trusted profiles directly from their message.

  3. 3. Travel Together

    Be at your planned meeting point on time. Should you be delayed, send a quick message letting your co-travellers know. You can easily recognise your co-travellers thanks to their member profile picture.

    Add a photo of your car to your own profile: it'll help your co-travellers find you at the meeting point.

  4. 4. Your Co-travellers Pay You

    Your co-travellers will pay you the agreed contribution during the journey. It might be useful to have a bit of change on you.

    After travelling together leave a rating for your co-travellers, they will return the favour and it will boost your Experience Level, helping you to find more co-travellers in future!
